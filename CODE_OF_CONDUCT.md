# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want this project to be a welcoming, respectful, and constructive space for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and patient
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly
- Give actionable feedback
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior, not people
- Help keep discussions collaborative and useful

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or limit participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the repository maintainer privately.
